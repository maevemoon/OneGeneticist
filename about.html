WERONIKA SWISTOWSKA

I am a currently studying a BSc (Honours) in Genetics at University College Dublin. I will graduate in 2025.



I programmed a little bit as a curious 12 year old, mainly to try to make silly plugins for my favourite video game at the time, Minecraft. But I was always one to move around hobbies quickly, and so it was an activity I picked up very irregularly, kind of like an old show you enjoy but watch once every few months at best.

Back in university, I picked up the hobby again, for longer this time. I finished a short, online course titled "Bioinformatics for Beginners" by UC San Diego, and that introduced me to a whole new world of coding. Since then, I have attempted bioinformatics-related challenges such as ROSALIND's Bioinformatics Stronghold, tried to build my own website (this one!), and also did some undergraduate research in bioinformatics!
